# Teacher Lesson Internalization Protocol



**Purpose**


Reading the lesson ensures that teachers understand the
content they will be teaching, as well as the progression of
learning throughout the lesson.


Step 1: **Understand the lesson purpose and objectives.**








**•** **Use the Lesson:** Read the Primary Focus objectives of the lesson and aligned TEKS; complete the Formative
Assessments; read the Observation and Check for Understanding sections.


Step 2: **Understand the sequence and pacing of activities.**

**•** **Use the Lesson at a Glance:** Read the Lesson at a Glance. Familiarize yourself with the pacing of the lesson.


Step 3: **Prepare to teach each activity with an activity deep dive.**

**•** **Use the Lesson:** Read the lesson. Practice activities such as chaining, dictation, and manipulation
movements.


Step 4: **Organize your resources.**

**•** **Use the Advance Preparation section:** Prepare your resources, including observational charts from
Teacher Resources.
