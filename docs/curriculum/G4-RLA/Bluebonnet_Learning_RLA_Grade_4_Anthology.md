# **Bluebonnet Learning Reading Language** **Arts (RLA) Grade 4 Anthology**

The anthology is intended for use with the following Bluebonnet Learning RLA Grade 4 units:

   - Unit 1: Personal Narratives

   - Unit 3: Contemporary Fiction: Letters from Heaven

   - Unit 7: Poetry: Wondrous Words

The anthology contains works from numerous third-party copyright owners that are provided
for use in the anthology under a limited license, which does not permit digital distribution. As
such, these works are not available in the Bluebonnet Learning Portal. The following must be
sourced independently or through EMAT print orders (for school districts).


**Excerpted books include:**

_Small Steps: The Year I Got Polio_ by Peg Kehret | ISBN: 9780807574584

_Letters from Heaven_ by Lydia Gil | ISBN: 9781558857988


**Poems include:**

“Wonderful World”

“Wishes” by Norman Ault

“My First Memory (of Librarians)” by Nikki Giovanni

“Harlem (2)” by Langston Hughes

“I Hear America Singing” by Walt Whitman

“The Delight Song of Tsoai-talee” by N. Scott Momaday

“Words Free As Confetti” by Pat Mora

“Fog” by Carl Sandburg

“Casey at the Bat” by Ernest Lawrence Thayer

“Kavikanthabharana” (“A Poet Should Learn With His Eyes”) by Kshemendra
translated into the English language by W. S. Merwin
