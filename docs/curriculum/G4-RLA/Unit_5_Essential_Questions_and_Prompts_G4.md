# Grade 4
## Unit 5: American Revolution: Building a Nation

### Unit-level Essential Question
#### How can a colony or group of colonies become a country?

Lessons 1–5


Guiding Question: How might conflicts lead to war?


Writing Prompt: What are some of the big events that led to discontent in
the colonies?


Lessons 6–10


Guiding Question: How can a disadvantage be turned into an advantage?


Writing Prompt: How did the Continental Army win the war, even though it
was at a disadvantage?


Lessons 11–16


Guiding Question: What role did women have in the Revolutionary War?


Writing Prompt: What else can you learn about the female heroes of the
Revolutionary War? Choose one woman from the reading and research to
find out more about her life.


Unit 5 Culminating Activity


Imagine you are a soldier in the Continental Army, marching from battle to
battle in the cold. Write a letter back home to your family with details about
what battles you have fought and what the conditions are like in the camps.
