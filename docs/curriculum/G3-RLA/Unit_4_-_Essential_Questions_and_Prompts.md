# Grade 3
## Unit 4: Ancient Rome

### Unit-level Essential Question
#### How did Rome expand into an empire?

Lessons 1–3


Guiding Question: What were the characteristics of the mythical Roman
gods and goddesses?


Writing Prompt: Choose two of the mythical Roman gods/goddesses and
explain how they are similar and different.


Lessons 4–8


Guiding Question: Why was Julius Caesar one of Rome’s most influential
leaders?


Writing Prompt: How did Julius Caesar feel about technology? What was
his preferred way of using it?


Lessons 9–12


Guiding Question: What led to the collapse of the Roman Empire?


Writing Prompt: Why would a book like _Justinian’s Code_ be important for
an empire like Rome?


Unit 4 Culminating Activity


Should the Roman Empire have stayed in one piece, or was it a good idea to
split into the Western and Eastern Empires? In this speech, you will be one
of the leaders of Rome, and you need to convince your fellow leaders (your
class) of your opinion. How will you persuade them to stay in one piece or
split up?
