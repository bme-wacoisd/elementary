# Teacher Lesson Internalization Protocol







Step 1: **Understand the lesson purpose and objectives.**

**•** **Use the esson:** eview the Primary Focus Objectives and consider how they connect to the TEKS and ELPS; read
all texts or readers that are part of the lesson; complete the Formative Assessment; complete the Check for
Understanding found within the lesson; and identify and record the literacy skill(s), knowledge and/or vocabulary
students will need to be successful on the Formative Assessment.


Step 2: **Understand the sequence and pacing of activities.**

**•** **Use the esson:** amiliarize yourself with the pacing of the lesson; review each lesson component and its allotted
time.


Step 3: **Prepare to teach each activity with an activity deep dive.**

**•** **Use the lesson** dentify and record the alignment between the Formative Assessment, CFUs and the Primary
Focus Objective.

**•** **Use the side bars throughout the lesson:** dentify and annotate the embedded supports in the lesson that can
be leveraged for diverse learners (emergent bilingual, gifted and talented, and students with disabilities).


Step 4: **Organize your resources.**

**•** **Use the Advance Preparation section** found in the lesson to prepare your resources.
