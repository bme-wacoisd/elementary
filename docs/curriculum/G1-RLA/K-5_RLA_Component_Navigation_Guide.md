## **Welcome!**

Bluebonnet Learning K–5 Reading Language Arts has been specifically designed by the Texas Education
Agency (TEA) for students served by Texas schools. These high-quality instructional materials reflect
research-based instructional strategies for literacy, including explicit, systematic practice with
foundational skills, complex grade-level texts, knowledge coherence, and text-based responses. The
units strive to engage all learners with diverse content while equipping teachers with the rich
literature, lessons, and resources needed to make learning impactful for every student.


The K–5 Reading Language Arts Team

**Get to Know the Program**


Bluebonnet Learning K–5 Reading Language Arts has been specifically designed by the Texas Education
Agency (TEA) for students served by Texas schools. The units strive to engage all learners with diverse
content while equipping teachers with the rich literature, lessons, and resources needed to make
learning impactful for every student.


This guide will help you in the review process:


- Get an overview of the program by browsing the **[Program and Implementation](https://texasgateway.org/)**
**Guide** .

- Use the **Component Navigation Guide** to understand the components of the instructional materials
and how they are organized.

## **Getting to Know the Program: Product Components**

#### **Reading Language Arts Unit Components**


The following materials come in **Reading Language Arts Teacher Kits** . The materials provided in each kit
vary by grade and unit.



Print Materials

  - Teacher Guides

  - Flip Books

  - Image Card Sets

  - Authentic Text Collections

  - Reader Information

  - Unit-Specific Special Materials



Digital Materials

  - Family Support Letters

  - Digital Components

  - Essential Questions and Prompts

  - Fluency Supplements

  - Pacing and Scope & Sequence Guide
