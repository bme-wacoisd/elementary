# **Bluebonnet Learning Reading Language** **Arts (RLA) Grade 5 Anthology**

The anthology is intended for use with the following Bluebonnet Learning RLA Grade 5 units:

   - Unit 1: Personal Narratives

   - Unit 4: _Don Quixote_ : A Hopeful Knight’s Tale

   - Unit 5: Poetry: A Collage of Words

   - Unit 9: Juneteenth and Beyond

The anthology contains works from numerous third-party copyright owners that are provided for
use in the anthology under a limited license, which does not permit digital distribution. As such,
these works are not available in the Bluebonnet Learning Portal. The following must be sourced
independently or through EMAT print orders (for school districts).


**Excerpted books include:**

_Rosa Parks: My Story_ by Rosa Parks with Jim Haskins | ISBN: 9780141301204

_Step By Step: A Memoir of Hope, Friendship, Perseverance, and Living the American Dream_ by
Bertie Bowman | ISBN: 9780345504333

_Adventures of Don Quixote_ by Argentina Palacios | ISBN: 9780486407913

_Letter from Birmingham Jail_ by Martin Luther King, Jr. | ISBN: 9780241339466


**Poems include:**
