# Bluebonnet Learning Curriculum

Texas-aligned K-5 foundational skills curriculum materials, converted to markdown for easy reading and AI-assisted lesson planning.

## About This Site

This site hosts curriculum materials from [Bluebonnet Learning](https://www.bluebonnetlearning.com/), Texas's open educational resources for K-5 foundational literacy skills. The materials are aligned to the Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS) standards.

The original PDF materials have been converted to markdown format to enable:

- **Easy browsing** - Read curriculum materials directly in your browser
- **AI-assisted planning** - Load materials into Claude, ChatGPT, or other AI assistants
- **Full-text search** - Find specific content across all curriculum materials
- **Accessibility** - Screen reader friendly text format

## Curriculum Contents

The curriculum includes materials for:

- **Kindergarten** - Foundational literacy skills
- **Grade 1** - Foundational skills and reading comprehension
- **Grade 2** - Foundational skills and reading comprehension
- **Grade 3** - Reading, writing, and foundational skills
- **Grade 4** - Reading and writing
- **Grade 5** - Reading and writing

Each grade level includes:
- Unit guides and lesson plans
- Student workbooks and activity sheets
- Teacher guides and instructional materials
- Assessment materials
- Family support letters (English and Spanish)
